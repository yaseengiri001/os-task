=== DEMO 2: Race condition, mutex and counting semaphore ===
(A) 4 threads each increment one shared counter 1000000 times.
    Expected final value = 4000000

    WITHOUT mutex : 1046018    <-- WRONG: updates lost to the race
    WITH mutex    : 4000000    <-- correct, every increment counted

(B) Counting semaphore: at most 2 of 6 threads inside at once.
    thread 1 entered  (active now = 1)
    thread 2 entered  (active now = 2)
    thread 1 leaving  (active now = 2)
    thread 2 leaving  (active now = 1)
    thread 3 entered  (active now = 1)
    thread 4 entered  (active now = 2)
    thread 3 leaving  (active now = 2)
    thread 4 leaving  (active now = 1)
    thread 5 entered  (active now = 1)
    thread 6 entered  (active now = 2)
    thread 5 leaving  (active now = 2)
    thread 6 leaving  (active now = 1)
    Peak concurrent threads in the region = 2 (limit was 2) OK

=== DEMO 4: Deadlock demonstration and prevention ===
Two threads make OPPOSITE transfers between two locked accounts -
the classic deadlock trigger.

  [Prevention 1] Lock ordering (lock lower account id first):
    finished - no deadlock.
    balances: acct0=1000  acct1=1000  total=2000 (started at 2000)

  [Prevention 2] Trylock + backoff (never wait holding a lock):
    finished - no deadlock.
    balances: acct0=1000  acct1=1000  total=2000 (started at 2000)
    trylock backoffs handled: 57644

  Both preventions kept the books correct AND never deadlocked.
  Menu option 5 runs the UNSAFE version, which hangs on purpose.
